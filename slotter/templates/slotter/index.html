Hello!

Slotter is a scheduling app.

When you need to divide people into smaller groups for meetings, Slotter can help with finding times that work for everyone (but especially you) and assigning them to groups.

Once you've collected everyone's schedules into a .csv and uploaded it, all you need to do is:

  1. Specify a couple of basic parameters (e.g. how many groups do you want? how small can groups be?)
  2. Pick the times that make sense to you
Slotter will do the rest — giving you all of the options for assigning everyone to a time. And if you want to have more of a hand in how people are grouped, Slotter can help with that too.

Check things out for yourself by clicking schedule and playing around with some of the pre-loaded data. This sample data comes from Slotter's original use case — dividing classes of college students into smaller seminar groups while working around their existing class schedules.
